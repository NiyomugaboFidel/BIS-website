# BAHO Health Intelligence

An AI-powered system designed to improve early disease detection, patient support, and public health decision-making, starting in Rwanda.

---

## 🚀 Overview

BAHO HI is a dual-layer digital health platform that connects individual patient data with population-level health intelligence.

The system aims to:
- Detect diseases earlier
- Support patients with AI-assisted triage
- Provide real-time insights for healthcare providers and governments
- Strengthen health systems in low-resource environments

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

BAHO HI combines:

### 1. Patient-Facing Mobile Application
- AI-powered symptom triage (non-diagnostic)
- Health guidance and education
- Appointment assistance
- Medication reminders

### 2. Health Intelligence Dashboard
- Disease trend monitoring
- Early outbreak detection
- Data visualization for decision-makers
- Public health alerts

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Users input symptoms through the mobile app  
2. The system analyzes inputs using AI + rule-based logic  
3. A risk level is generated (low, moderate, high)  
4. Users receive guidance or are referred to healthcare professionals  
5. Aggregated anonymized data is processed  
6. Insights are displayed on a dashboard for health authorities  

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Healthcare systems in many regions face:

- Late disease detection
- Fragmented or non-digital patient data
- Limited access to healthcare services
- Weak early warning systems for outbreaks

BAHO HI addresses these challenges through data-driven, AI-supported solutions.

## 🔒 Ethics & Safety

- The system does NOT provide final medical diagnoses  
- Human healthcare professionals remain in the loop  
- Data is anonymized and handled responsibly  
- Designed to minimize bias and ensure fairness

## 🌍 Vision
